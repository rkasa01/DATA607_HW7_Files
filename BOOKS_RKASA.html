The Man Who Mistook His Wife for a Hat Oliver Sacks I enjoy the writing style of Oliver Sacks. This is a book of interesting case studies. This book was gifted to me by one of my former mentors.
Clinical Psychopharmacology Made Ridiculously Simple John Preston, James Johnson I used this book through a challenging Psychopharmacology course in college. It's written in a humorous style. I got an A in the class!
When Breath Becomes Air Paul Kalanithi This is an autobiography by neurosurgeon, Dr. Paul Kalanithi, with worsening lung cancer. It helped me navigate the death of a loved one with cancer. It's one of the most beautiful books I have ever read and is very special to me.
